Online Food Delivery Business Analytics System

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing Business Intelligence and Performance Analysis for an online food delivery platform using SQL. The analysis covers customer behavior, restaurant performance, revenue trends, delivery efficiency, and product-level insights to support data-driven business decision making.

The project simulates a real-world relational database environment and demonstrates how SQL can be used to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

🎯 Business Objectives

Analyze revenue performance and discount impact

Understand customer ordering behavior and retention patterns

Identify top-performing restaurants and cuisines

Evaluate product-level sales and basket trends

Monitor delivery efficiency and operational performance

Generate insights to support strategic decision-making

🗂️ Database Schema

The project uses a relational database consisting of the following tables:

Customers – Customer demographics and signup details

Restaurants – Restaurant information, cuisine, ratings, and location

Orders – Transactional order data including payment method, discount, and delivery time

Order_Item – Item-level breakdown of each order

Delivery Agents – Delivery personnel details and performance ratings

📊 Key Analysis Performed

Total and city-wise revenue analysis

Customer segmentation and repeat purchase behavior

Restaurant and cuisine performance ranking

Most frequently ordered food items

Payment method preference analysis

Delivery time and operational efficiency insights

⚙️ Advanced SQL Concepts Used

Complex Joins & Aggregations

Subqueries and Grouping

Views for reusable business metrics

Stored Procedures for dynamic reporting

Indexing for performance optimization

Triggers for automation and data integrity

🚀 Performance Optimization & Automation

Indexed frequently queried columns (order_date, customer name)

Created revenue view for simplified reporting

Implemented stored procedure for Top N restaurant retrieval

Developed triggers for:

High-value order logging

Delivery delay monitoring

Prevention of invalid discount values

📈 Key Business Insights

Net revenue remains strong despite discount utilization

Repeat customers significantly contribute to platform revenue

A small group of restaurants drives major revenue share

Certain cuisines show consistently high demand

Delivery time variability highlights operational improvement opportunities
